The Best Movies According to Jason

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Blazing Saddles

This is classic movie where those who are sensitive to words shouldn't watch.
The acting, the intense humor and the overall scope of the movie poking fun at all different people is what makes this movie great.
Mel Brooks is a comedy genius.

SpaceBalls

A Star Wars parody that lives up to the spoof reputation.
Instead of Han and Chewie, you've got Lonestar and Barf (a Mog) a down and out duo trying to make money in the galaxy.
They roped into trying to save the princess who happens to be Druish. Mel Brooks again pushes the limits of his comedies.

The whole Mission Impossible series.

A series of action adventure stories that very well cross into each other.
Shows an agent and his team saving the world multiple times as he ages.
This series is top notch action and adventure.
